About us

Why research on mental health is improtant

We provide a place to register interest to take part in mental health research.

Currently, there is an enormous pressure on healthcare systems due to increased demand for mental health services. Mental health research plays a vital role in deepening our understanding of various mental illnesses, their causes, risk factors, and the underlying mechanisms. This knowledge helps in developing effective prevention strategies, diagnostic tools, and treatment interventions. For research to advance, greater participation in studies is crucial.

Why to sign up

It is estimated that approximately 1 in 4 people in the UK experience a mental health problem each year.

Mental health issues among young people are a growing concern. About 1 in 8 children and young people in the UK have a diagnosable mental health disorder, and there has been an increase in the prevalence of mental health issues among students in higher education.

By signing up, you will be able to:

• Find the studies that match your information.

• Connect with researchers to show an interest in their studies.

• Discover exciting new studies.

• Help us improve mental health care and treatment.
